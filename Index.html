Cola is a carbonated soft drink flavored with vanilla, cinnamon, citrus oils and other flavorings.
Cola became popular worldwide after the American pharmacist John Stith Pemberton invented Coca-Cola, a trademarked brand, in 1886, which was imitated by other manufacturers. Most colas contain caffeine originally from the kola nut.
